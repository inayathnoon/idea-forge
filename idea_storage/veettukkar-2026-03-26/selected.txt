SELECTED DIRECTION: Job-Request Marketplace
==========================================

Direction Number: 2
Selected On: 2026-03-26

DESCRIPTION
-----------
Homeowners post a job request specifying: skill category (one of 6), date, duration (half-day
or full-day), location (GPS or area name), and a short description. Workers within ~10km who
have that skill and are available on that date receive a push notification. They can accept or
decline. The homeowner is notified when a worker accepts, and receives the worker's phone number
to coordinate final details. After the job, both sides rate each other.

An "available today" toggle lets workers signal same-day availability, surfacing them at the
top of relevant searches for last-minute requests — borrowing urgency value from Direction 6
without requiring a separate product mode.

WHY THIS OVER THE DEFAULT (same)
---------------------------------
This IS the default direction, closest to the raw idea. No non-default reasoning needed.

PRODUCT PRINCIPLES FROM THIS DIRECTION
----------------------------------------
- Cash payment is preserved; the app handles matching only, not money.
- Trust is built through ratings, not certification (faster to bootstrap).
- Malayalam UI is mandatory from day one; it's not a localisation afterthought.
- Worker profiles are simple (skills, location, rate, rating) — no portfolio, no CV.
- Homeowners are verified by phone number only; no ID check for MVP.

WHAT THIS DIRECTION DEFERS
---------------------------
- In-app payments and commission model (v2)
- Worker certification or background checks (v2)
- Subscription-based homeowner plans (v3)
- Expansion beyond Kerala (post-PMF)
