# Ask Questions

Generate **5–7 precise clarifying questions** to understand what the user is talking about.

## Focus areas

1. **Problem** — What exact pain does this solve? For whom?
2. **Users** — Who uses this? What is their context?
3. **Solution** — What does it actually *do*?
4. **Scope** — What's in MVP vs later?
5. **Market** — Which geography, industry, or segment?
6. **Success** — How do you know it worked?

Do NOT ask about tech stack, language, or infrastructure — those are decided during scaffolding, not idea exploration.

Present conversationally. Wait for answers.
