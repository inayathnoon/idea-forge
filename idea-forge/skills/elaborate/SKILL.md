# Elaborate

When the user shares a raw idea and something is unclear or missing, **ask them to elaborate** before moving on.

## Instructions

- Don't assume — get the gaps filled in
- Identify what's vague, missing, or assumed
- Ask specifically for those parts
- Wait for their answer before proceeding
