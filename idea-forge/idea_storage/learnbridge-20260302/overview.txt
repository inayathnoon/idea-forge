LearnBridge — Direction Exploration
Date: 2026-03-02

ORIGINAL IDEA
Children studying alone (CBSE 5th, ~10–11 years) get stuck on algebra. Parents are unavailable during study hours. App detects stuck signals, nudges the child, then alerts the parent with guided coaching prompts.

10 DIRECTIONS EXPLORED
1. Direct-to-Consumer B2C Subscription — Web + iOS parent app, monthly subscription
2. B2B School License — Sell to schools, teachers get alerts
3. WhatsApp-Native Parent Alerts — No iOS app, alerts via WhatsApp ← SELECTED
4. AI Tutor (No Parent Loop) — AI helps child directly, no parent needed
5. Tuition Center White-Label — B2B2C via coaching institutes
6. Full CBSE Curriculum Platform — All subjects, grades 1–8
7. Gamified Peer Learning — Social layer, badges, leaderboards
8. Offline-First Regional Language App — Rural India, SMS alerts
9. YouTube + Parent Community — Free content, paid community app
10. Parenting Confidence Platform — Product primarily for parents

TOP 3 CONSIDERED
- Direction 1: B2C Subscription (closest to original vision)
- Direction 3: WhatsApp Alerts (lowest friction for parents in India)
- Direction 4: AI Tutor (different product philosophy)

SELECTED
Direction 3 — WhatsApp-Native Parent Alerts
User chose this for lower adoption friction. WhatsApp is near-universal in India. No iOS install required.
