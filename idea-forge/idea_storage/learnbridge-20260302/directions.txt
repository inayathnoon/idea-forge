10 Directions — LearnBridge

1. Direct-to-Consumer B2C Subscription
   Web app for child + iOS app for parent. Stuck detection → 1-min nudge → parent iOS alert with guided coaching prompts. CBSE 5th algebra. Monthly subscription.

2. B2B School License
   Sell to CBSE schools. Teachers get the stuck alerts instead of parents. School admin dashboard shows class-wide patterns. Subscription per school.

3. WhatsApp-Native Parent Alerts
   Replace the iOS app entirely. Parent gets stuck alerts + guided prompts via WhatsApp. No app install required — dramatically higher reach in India.

4. AI Tutor (No Parent Loop)
   When the child is stuck, an AI tutor talks the child through the specific stuck point interactively. Parent receives a summary. No iOS app needed.

5. Tuition Center White-Label
   Sell to existing tuition centers and coaching institutes. They brand it, use it for between-session homework support. B2B2C model.

6. Full CBSE Curriculum Platform
   Start with algebra but architect for all subjects and grades 1–8. Higher TAM, longer retention, more LTV per family.

7. Gamified Peer Learning
   Add social/competitive layer — badges, leaderboards, friends' progress. Child intrinsic motivation goes up. Freemium consumer app.

8. Offline-First Regional Language App
   Target rural/semi-urban India. Works offline, Hindi + regional language support, SMS-based parent alerts (no iOS dependency).

9. YouTube + Parent Community
   Free animated content on YouTube, monetize via a parent community app — parenting tips, homework coaching, expert Q&A.

10. Parenting Confidence Platform
    Primary product is for parents: teach parents how to coach kids through any subject struggle. Child app is secondary.
